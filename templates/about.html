About Me

I advocate a straightforward approach as a high school college counselor: the focus must be finding the best particular path for each student. In some cases, this means pursuing admission at the country’s most elite private universities. In other cases, this means searching for just the right quirky liberal arts college. Sometimes this means starting off in community college, and sometimes this means taking one or two gap years in exploration of other possibilities.

Every child is different. My commitment is to find and understand these differences.

Listed here is a selection of the services I offer:
Optimizing high school course selection.
Honing extracurricular activities to help your competitiveness.
Walking through the college application process, from the big picture (which schools are good fits and realistic targets) to the fine details (how to approach the college essay).
Uncovering assistance available through the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).

Prior to my work as an independent college counselor, I spent 15 years as a high school college counselor in Portland Public Schools. I worked with at-risk youth populations, and, for the bulk of my career, I worked in the competitive halls of Lincoln High School.

I’m the mother of two grown sons, and so consider myself personally close to this moment of transition. Please get in touch if you’d like to learn more about how I might help.
